Market Analysis

Current trends and opportunities in the CPG/Food and beverage industry

Industry Challenges

CPG brands face increasing pressure to understand consumer sentiment quickly as market conditions evolve rapidly. Traditional research methods are too slow and expensive for today's pace of business.

Market Opportunity

There is significant demand for fast, affordable consumer insights that can be delivered within compressed timelines and budgets, especially for small to mid-sized CPG brands in high-growth segments.

Competitive Gap

Most agencies offer either traditional research (slow, expensive) or automated tools (fast, shallow). Few combine AI-powered analysis with human strategic interpretation for the best of both worlds.

Service Offering

Rapid Consumer Sentiment Analysis combines AI-powered insights with strategic interpretation

Service Overview

Rapid Consumer Sentiment Analysis delivers deep emotional and linguistic insights from consumer feedback in just 6 days. By combining AI-moderated mini-qual interviews with dual-layer analysis of both voice emotion and language, we uncover the hidden drivers behind consumer behavior.

Our unique approach pairs cutting-edge AI technology with human strategic interpretation, delivering actionable recommendations that go beyond what either humans or AI could achieve alone.

Technology Stack

Our integrated technology solution combines best-in-class tools for comprehensive analysis

Voiceform

Core Platform

Handles survey distribution and voice interviews with auto-transcription capabilities and conditional logic for personalized interviews.

Hume AI

Emotion Analysis

Industry-leading emotion recognition through their Empathic Voice Interface (EVI) API, providing nuanced emotional context from voice recordings.

Gemini API

Text Analysis

Advanced language model for deep semantic analysis, theme extraction, pattern recognition, and insight generation from transcribed interviews.

Airtable

Data Storage

Flexible database structure for centralized storage of responses, analysis results, and reporting, with built-in automation capabilities.

Insight7

Visualization & Reporting

AI-powered visualization of open-ended responses, extracting insights from interviews and identifying patterns across responses.
